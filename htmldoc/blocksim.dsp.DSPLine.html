Bases : object

Generic DSP line

Paramètres

  • name – Name of the line

  • samplingStart – First x coord of the sample of the line

  • samplingPeriod (Hz) – x coord spacing of the line

  • y_serie – Complex samples of the line

  • projection – Axe projection. Can be “rectilinear” or “polar”

  • default_transform – Function to apply to the samples before plotting. Shall be vectorized
