blocksim.dsp.ActiveAntenna module

class blocksim.dsp.ActiveAntenna.ActiveAntenna(ac, dtype=<class 'numpy.complex128'>)[source]

Bases : blocksim.core.Node.AComputer

Active antenna implementation

The inputs of the computer are tx_pos, rx_pos and tx_sig The outputs of the computer are rx_sig

The tx_pos and rx_pos vectors represent a 3D position (m) and 3D velocity (m/s) in ITRF tx_sig is the RX signal

Paramètres

  • name – Name of the computer

  • freq (Hz) – Frequency of the antenna

  • hpbw (rad) – Half Power Beam Width of the antenna

  • th_profile – Function that associated to an off-axis angle the distance between the antenna and the receiver

  • win – The window to apply to the antenna
